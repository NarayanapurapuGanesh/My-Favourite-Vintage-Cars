Ford Mustang 1967 The 1967 Mustang is available with 13 engine- transmission combinations ranging from the economical 200 Six with a three-speed manual transmission to the powerful 390 4v GT V-8 with four-on-the-floor. Dodge Challenger One of the fun facts about the Dodge Challenger is that is was among the first car models to feature a Hemi engine. Hemi engines are hemispherical-shaped engines with round-headed pistons. The unique design is responsible for lesser heat loss and improved acceleration.
